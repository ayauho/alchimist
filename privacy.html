What Alchimist does

Alchimist is an AI content tool. When you trigger it, it sends the page content and any text you select to an AI service to generate posts, replies, articles, image prompts, and related outputs. Generated content is shown to you; you decide whether to use it.

Data we process

  • Text sent for generation — the content of the page you are on, plus any text you select, at the moment you invoke Alchimist.
  • Your settings — personas, presets, generated outputs, feature toggles, consent choices, and granted sites, stored locally via chrome.storage. The unlimitedStorage permission is used solely to hold larger local libraries (persona roster, saved outputs) on your device.
  • Your AI provider API key — stored locally in chrome.storage so requests can be made on your behalf. The raw key is never transmitted to us.

We do not collect browsing history. Alchimist does not run on a site until you click its icon (activeTab) or grant that site explicitly.

Where processing happens

Alchimist does not run a local AI model. To generate any output, the text described above is sent over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection directly from your browser to your configured AI provider (Google Gemini API, generativelanguage.googleapis.com), authenticated with your own API key. This is required for the extension to function — there is no on-device-only mode.

There is no developer-controlled server. Requests go directly from your browser to the AI provider, and are subject to that provider's privacy policy. Alchimist does not receive, proxy, store, sell, or share the text you submit. It does not use your data for advertising.

Your controls

  • Grant or revoke per-site access at any time.
  • Remove or change the AI provider API key you configure.
  • Clear stored settings from the extension options.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. The text you submit is sent to the AI provider you configure (using your own API key) solely to generate the requested output. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

Changes

Material changes to this policy will be surfaced in the extension before they take effect.

Consent

On first run, the extension shows a consent notice before any page text is sent to your AI provider. You may revoke consent at any time in settings; the extension will not generate content without it.

Host permissions

The extension requests access to a specific website only when you ask it to act on that site, via a standard browser permission prompt. Permissions are granted per site, on demand. You can revoke per-site access at any time in your browser's extension settings.

Premium licensing

Premium features are unlocked with a one-time license key purchased externally through getly.store, a third-party commerce platform. No payment processing occurs within this extension or on any developer-controlled server.

Purchase flow: Clicking the buy button inside the extension opens the Getly product page in your browser. You complete the transaction directly on Getly's platform. Your payment card details are entered on and processed by Getly — the developer never receives, sees, intercepts, stores, or has access to your credit card number, billing address, or any payment credentials at any point. This data exists solely between you and Getly.

What the developer receives: Only a payment confirmation and the email address you used at checkout, solely for the purpose of delivering your license key. Nothing else is transmitted to the developer.

Getly's data practices: Getly's handling of your payment and personal data is governed exclusively by Getly's own privacy policy. The developer has no control over and no visibility into Getly's data practices beyond what Getly publicly discloses.

License key delivery: Your unique license key is delivered to your purchase email within 24 hours of confirmed payment. To activate premium features, open the extension, go to Settings — License, and enter your email and license key. Activation is instant and requires no internet connection — it is verified entirely on your device.

License validation: Performed entirely on your device using local cryptography (ECDSA P-256). No activation request, license check, usage ping, or any data is ever transmitted to the developer or any external server after activation. The raw license key is never stored locally — only a one-way cryptographic hash is retained, solely to confirm activation state.

Refunds: Refund requests are handled directly through Getly per their refund policy. The developer does not process refunds independently.
